CRA Vehicle Use Requirements

📋

What the CRA Requires

For Business Vehicle Use

  • ✓
    Complete logbook recording all business trips with date, destination, purpose, and exact kilometers
  • ✓
    Total kilometers driven for business vs. personal use (percentage calculation required)
  • ✓
    Starting/ending odometer readings for each business trip
  • ✓
    Records must be kept for 6 years after filing
  • ✓
    Businesses can deduct expenses only for the business-use percentage

For Personal Vehicle Used for Business

  • ✓
    Same detailed logbook requirements as business vehicles
  • ✓
    Must separate commuting (not deductible) from business travel (deductible)
  • ✓
    CRA allows "simplified logbook" method after keeping a full logbook for one complete year
  • ✓
    Even with simplified method, you must track business mileage for a three-month sample period annually

Penalties for Non-Compliance

  • ✓
    Denied deductions for all vehicle expenses
  • ✓
    Potential reassessment of previous tax years
  • ✓
    Interest charges on unpaid taxes
  • ✓
    Penalties of up to 50% of understated tax in cases of gross negligence
