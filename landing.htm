Our services

At ClearingStation Ltd., we offer a wide range of services to help your business thrive. Our experienced team can handle all of your bookkeeping needs, including categorizing income and expenses, reconciling bank accounts, and managing accounts payables and receivables. We also provide monthly financial reports and analysis to help you stay on top of your business's finances. Plus, we can assist with tax preparation, sales tax filings, and corporate T2 filings. And, if you need help with payroll and source deductions, we've got you covered there too.

  • Dedicated bookkeeper
  • Year-end financial statements
  • Monthly bookkeeping
  • Payroll
  • Issuance of T4
  • Government communication
  • Audit assistance
  • GST / PST filing
  • Issuance of T2

But our services go beyond just bookkeeping and taxes. We are also here to provide expert guidance and support to help your business succeed. Our team will work closely with you to understand your unique situation and provide personalized recommendations to help you make informed decisions about your finances. We are committed to providing exceptional customer service and are always here to answer any questions you may have.
