Jennifer Flaming, VP

Jennifer Flaming brings a decade of expertise to her role as Vice President at ClearingStation Ltd., having directly managed nearly a hundred CRA audits in her career. This experience has deepened her understanding of complex financial regulations and the unexpected challenges businesses can face from auditor inquiries at any time.

In 2020, CRA audits cost small businesses an average of $137,000 and medium-sized businesses $338,000—risks no business owner can afford to ignore.

Over the years she has become a trusted partner to clients from a wide range of industries, including medical practices, physicians, lawyers, financial advisors, and tech companies.
