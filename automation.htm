A Simple, Flexible and Powerful Dashboard

By logging into your dashboard, you will have a comprehensive view of all your financial transactions from all companies and investments, in one convenient location.

For example, if you have rental properties, you can see each property's transactions and easily open the coresponding invoice with one click. Our proof-based accounting practice will highlight the number on the invoice or receipt.

The same applies to all of your companies accounts and investments, giving you a complete overview of your financials at all times. For example, with our soft-real-time practice, you can print an Income Statement and Balance Sheet at any moment.

Hosted in Winnipeg, our servers and state-of-the-art classifiers make meticulous record keeping and audits a breeze. But, our professional bookkeepers take a step further by checking and confirming the data, ensuring accuracy and taking into account your personal tax situation and strategies.

Secure and Reliable for Heavily Regulated Industries

ClearingStation provides secure solutions for heavily regulated industries in Canada, built on years of experience in banking, messaging, and accounting platform development.

We enforce strict policies to safeguard your data and never compromise on encryption key security. As an example, your files can only be viewed with your private key which is only shared in person when we set up your account, providing you with complete peace of mind.

Our high-speed, low-latency MQTT, TCP, UDP, and WebSocket communication channels are encrypted on top of SSL to ensure seamless and secure access.
