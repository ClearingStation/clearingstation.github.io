Why We Started This Service

Our clients were always busy, and paperwork was piling up, delaying bookkeeping catchup. When audit season comes around, every receipt and document is needed, many will spend weeks to find years worth of paperwork and in the worst case, never find it.

What We Found to Be True

  • Delaying Your Paperwork is Costly. Very Costly.

    When you hold off on delivering your paperwork to your accountant and bookkeeper, it is not just time you're wasting - it's money. Every day you wait, interest from the CRA piles up. Missed deadlines add penalties onto the interest owed, and lost receipts? Those are lost deductions, which means you're paying more in taxes then you technically should. Disorganization is costing businesses thousands, and in the worst case, a total loss of control in your life.

  • Documentation is Your Only Safety Net.

    Your documents aren't just pieces of paper—they're your safety net. They are the only thing that can give you the security and peace of mind when you're on the hook. That is why we store them securely in three locations, so whether it's a fire, flooding or another unexpected event, your important files are safe with triple redundancy, and you're covered.

  • You Don't Have Time for This.

    When you're running a business, you can't be worried about removing staples, scanning receipts and documents and sending them off to your bookkeeper or accountant. We take on the burden of scanning, storing and organizing because our clients never have the time to.

  • A Second Layer of Protection

    Cloud services like Google Drive, GMail, QuickBooks etc. are great—until they lock you out of your account by accident, without you ever getting access to your files and emails ever again. One lockout and your could lose everything. We make sure that never happens to you. With us, your documents are accessible for seven years, no matter what.

  • Control Should be at Your Fingertips

    Your documents are yours. You control who gets access to them and who doesn't. Share them with your accountant, send them to the auditor, or anyone you need—on your terms. Everything stays secure, organized, and accessible. You never share more than you explicitly consent to.

Why Scanning In-House Costs Too Much

Scanning isn't just tedious—it's frustrating and eats up your time. Here's why our clients don't do it themselves anymore.

It's too Time Consuming

Every sheet you place on the scanner. Every second spent waiting. Scanning eats into your precious time, hour by hour.

Long Receipts Jam Scanners

Receipts get folded, crumpled, and cut-off. Even $3000-scanners struggle with long receipts, turning every attempt into a headache.

Staples Are A Pain

Each staple has to be carefully removed in order to properly scan documents, risking tears and damage to important papers. It's a slow, frustrating process.

Disorganized Results

Even after scanning, keeping everything organized can feel impossible. You're left with a mess of files that are all over the place.

Phone Photos are Blurred

You snap a picture of a receipt with your phone, only to realize later it’s blurred, out of focus, and unusable. So you take another, but now the lighting and shadows are off.

Industrial Accuracy

In-house scanning eats up hours and risks costly mistakes—attachments cover details, pages are skipped, files are lost.
