Secure and Reliable for Heavily Regulated Industries

We enforce the highest security standards, and encrypt all communication channels and files on top of SSL while private encryption keys are only shared in person.

ClearingStation provides secure solutions for heavily regulated industries in Canada, built on years of experience in banking, messaging, and accounting platform development.

We enforce strict policies to safeguard your data and never compromise on encryption key security. As an example, your files can only be viewed with your private key which is only shared in person when we set up your account, providing you with complete peace of mind.

Our high-speed, low-latency MQTT, TCP, UDP, and WebSocket communication channels are encrypted on top of SSL to ensure seamless and secure access.
