Frequently Asked Questions

Does ClearingStation sync with accounting software like QuickBooks?

ClearingStation operates autonomously and does not integrate or synchronize financial data with external bookkeeping software. We utilize our own proprietary software to efficiently manage your financial records, eliminating the need for additional third-party software. In contrast to solutions like QuickBooks, which are geared towards do-it-yourself bookkeeping.

If you are transitioning from QuickBooks, we seamlessly adapt to your closing balances to ensure the continuity of your bookkeeping.

What exactly does my bookkeeper at ClearingStation do?

Your dedicated bookkeeper at ClearingStation takes care of multiple tasks, including reconciling your accounts, categorizing transactions, and generating your financial statements. They also make necessary adjustments to ensure your books comply with tax regulations. While your bookkeeper may occasionally seek your input on transaction categorization, our aim is to make bookkeeping as hassle-free as possible for you.

Moreover, you can schedule a call with your bookkeeper or send them a message whenever you wish. We provide this support without additional fees or hourly charges because we're always eager to discuss bookkeeping and your financial statements with you.

I'm behind on my bookkeeping and taxes. Can ClearingStation help me get caught up?

Absolutely! ClearingStation offers the flexibility to add Catch Up bookkeeping to a standard plan. Regardless of how far behind you are, even if it's been years, we can swiftly assist you in getting back on track. For clients who are several years behind on their bookkeeping and taxes, we provide comprehensive solutions to ensure you are up-to-date and in compliance.

How do I submit my documents to ClearingStation? Do I need to provide receipts?

You have the flexibility to choose how you submit your documents to ClearingStation. You can either upload the documents digitally, or share them with us if you already have a document management system, or opt for our convenient doorstep pickup service.

We want the document submission process to be as hassle-free and efficient for you as possible.

Here is a list of typical paperwork that may be needed for accurate bookkeeping:

  • Invoices of all incomes
  • Invoices for all expenses (e.g., utility bills, office supplies, services)
  • Expenses related to your home office (e.g., rent or mortgage statements, utilities)
  • Bank statements
  • Credit Card Statements
  • ... and more, depending on your specific transactions and business needs.

Where are ClearingStation's bookkeepers located, and what are their qualifications?

ClearingStation's bookkeepers are all located in Manitoba, Canada, near Winnipeg.

Each client's main point of contact at ClearingStation is a dedicated, professionally-trained, in-house bookkeeper based in the heart of Manitoba. Our team is characterized by their deep expertise with years of experience in accounting and finance, ensuring that you receive the highest standard of service.

In addition to that, your data is kept locally in our Winnipeg Datacenter, utilizing robust encryption on ClearingStation-owned powerful on-premises servers. This unique approach combines personalized service with state-of-the-art technology, all located right here in Manitoba.

This dynamic combination allows us to effortlessly process substantial volumes of financial data with precision and speed, ultimately resulting in quicker and more accurate book closing for your financial peace of mind.

Does ClearingStation support personal accounts used for business purposes?

If you have personal accounts that are also used for business, ClearingStation can work with you to ensure proper categorization and handling of transactions. Our goal is to help you maintain accurate financial records, whether they are for personal or business use.
